--- layout: post title: "#2 Clustering Algorithms" date: 2034-02-19 description: This is the quiz for the clustering algorithms class --- #2 Clustering algorithms
  • A widely used method for identifying co-expressed gene modules
  • Starts with a gene-gene co-expression network
  • Identify initial gene modules (clusters) via a form of hierarchical clustering that accounts for shared neighbors using a topological overlap measure
  • Eigengenes are used as a representative of gene expression profiles within a module
